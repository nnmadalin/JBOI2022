About

What?

Junior Balkan Olympiad in Informatics (JBOI) is a programming contest for secondary school students from countries around or close to the Baltic sea. About 60 contestants from 10 countries compete against each other, solving difficult problems of algorithmic nature. Each participating country sends 6 contestants from their national olympiads which take place in the months beforehand.

Where?

This year, Junior Balkan Olympiad in Informatics takes place in Romania, more precisely in Dorohoi, Botosani, at Regina Maria High School!

When?

Junior Balkan Olympiad in Informatics takes place between August 28 and September 3!
